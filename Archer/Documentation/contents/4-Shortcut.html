Shortcut

Tips about quick access to Archer operations.

  • Most parts of Archer accept drag and drop operation.
  • Most common commands in Archer have at least one keyboard shortcut, button's tooltip will show what it is.
  • Win + A : Show/Hide Archer. One of the only two fixed global hotkeys, for the purpose of minimizing hotkey interference.
  • Win + Shift + A : Enable/Disable gesture temporarily. One of the only two fixed global hotkeys.
  • Archer support command line. Syntax: [arrow_name] [argument list].
